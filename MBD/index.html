    Welcome to Maharashtra Biodiversity Board

    The Maharashtra State Biodiversity Board (MSBB) is an autonomous body under the Government of Maharashtra's Department of Environment and Climate Change. It is situated in the premises of the Maharashtra State Forest Department. As per the provision made in Section 22 of the Biological Diversity Act, 2002, the State Government of Maharashtra constituted the Maharashtra State Biodiversity Board in the year 2008 vide Notification No. WLP/1008/CR/147/F-1, dated: 02/01/2008. The Board works under the Chairmanship of Hon. Minister for Environment & Climate Change, with Dr. Eknath B. Shinde as the Member Secretary of the Board. The Board includes nine other actively involved Members, comprising five ex-officio members representing the concerned departments of the State Government.

    Our mission is Save Water, Animals, and Environment

    To play a pivotal role to effectively implement the Biological Diversity Act, 2002 and advise the Government to facilitate the identification of biodiversity in ecosystems, landscapes, heritage sites, sacred grooves, hotspots, and niche areas with focus on endemic, rare, endangered, and threatened species by demarcation, protection, conservation, and utilization.
